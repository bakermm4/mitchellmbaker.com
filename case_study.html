Discovery

What does the company/service provide?

Indigo Rose Tattoo is widely considered the best, most professional shop to visit for tattoos in Columbia, SC. The staff have a combined experience of over 40 years, and specialize in multiple tattooing styles. These styles include Traditional, Neo-traditional, Realism, Asian/Japanese, portraits, and lettering. The artists stay up to date on all tattooing processes and take cleanliness and safety extremely seriously. The staff are professional, friendly, and work hard to make your tattoo experience one that you will likely want to repeat.

Who is the target audience for the site?

The target audience for the website is anyone in the South Carolina area looking to get a tattoo from renowned artists, though it isn't uncommon for people to travel from afar to be tattooed by Indigo Rose.

What information/content is the audience looking for?

The audience is looking for infomation regarding the artists and their specialties in style. Most likely they will be interested in viewing examples of the artist's work. They will also be looking for information such as hours of operation and location.

Describe the Company’s Brand, Personality, and Culture

Indigo Rose has a laid-back, local vibe. Being Columbia’s finest tattoo shop, Indigo Rose seeks to connect with the community, and attract local and regional tattoo enthusiasts. The name describes a flower that is both beautiful and uncommon, much like the individual beauty of the work that is done by the shop.

What are the company’s primary goals/objectives?

Indigo Rose strives to provide the public with great tattoos and outstanding customer service. They also endeavor to educate the public on tattoo culture as it is still considered taboo in many social circles.

Responsivity

Responsive Web Design (RWD) is the design approach used to create websites that can be optimally viewed on any screen size with a minimum of resizing and scrolling. This allows the audience to enjoy the website on their phone, tablet, or desktop.

Sass

Sass is a scripting language that helps to simplify the coding experience for the developer. The language is interpreted into Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) through the use of a preprocessor such as Grunt or Gulp. Code written in Sass is much cleaner and easier to read.

CSS3

CSS3 is an abbreviation for Cascading Style Sheets, level 3. It is what gives the website it's style.

jQuery

jQuery is a cross-platform JavaScript library designed to simplify the client-side scripting of HTML. jQuery is the most popular JavaScript library in use today.
